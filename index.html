Education

1 What school and univesity did you attend?

I lived in North Miami, so I went to Highland Oaks Middle school and transferred to North Miami Beach High School in Grade 10. I then went to Harvard for my undergrad in economics and MBA.

2 What were a few of your achievements throughout your high school and university years?

In high school, I was apart of all of the acadademic clubs you could think of, such as The Nation Honour Society and Senior Class Executive Board. I was the sophomore class president and graduated with a 4.6 GPA. At Harvard, I graduated fist in my economics class and was awarded the John H. Williams prize. I earned my MBA in business administration, I feel very accomplished that I graduated without the help of the internet!

Work

3 Where did you work at before Facebook?

I previously worked as a research assistant under Lawrence Summers at World Bank. I knew him very closely throughout my years at Harvard as he was my senior thesis adviser. After receiving my MBA, I worked at McKinsey & Company, and later worked along Summers once again for 5 years at the US Treasure Department as the chief of staff. I then joined Google in 2001 to work there for 7 years as the vice president of global online sales and operations.

4 How did each job lead to the next? What experiences and knowledge did you gain that helped you achieve important roles in the company?

Each time I worked at a different company, the more I grew - knowledge and mentally wise. I became a better leader and was able to comfortably connect to all my coworkers and subordinates. I had real experience in the workforce and understood the business flow. Working as Summer's research assistant helped me land the role at the US Treasury Department as he knew I was a hard working person with a growing mindset.
